How to buy

Step 1

Install Trust Wallet / MetaMask

Install Trust Wallet/MetaMask On Your Phone Or Desktop And Follow Instructions Carefully To Create Your Multi-Chain Wallet.

Step 2

Deposit BNB to Trust Wallet

After Successfully Creating Your Wallet, Purchase BNB By Highlighting BNB and Pressing The BUY Button. Purchase Desired Amount Of BNB via Credit Card Or Bank Account.

Step 3

Visit Pancakeswap

Connect Your Wallet To Pancakeswap (https://pancakeswap.finance/swap) On Your Phone Or Desktop (Same Device As A Wallet) On Your Web Browser Go To https://pancakeswap.finance/swapand Click Connect Wallet.

Step 4

Exchange BNB for $ELONAI

Set Slippage To 12% In The Options Menu. Now You Can Swap BNB For ELONMUSKCEO . Once Youve Completed Your Swap Your Tokens Will Be Shown In Your Wallet.
